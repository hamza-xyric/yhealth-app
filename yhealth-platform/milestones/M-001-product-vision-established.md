# [M-001] Product Vision Established

## Summary
Established yHealth Platform as an AI-powered health coach covering fitness, nutrition, and wellbeing. Defined the product vision, target users, and core value proposition of a conversation-first health companion.

## Decisions Made

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Conversation-first design | Unique UX differentiator, accessible for all users |
| Three pillars: Fitness, Nutrition, Wellbeing | Comprehensive health coverage without medical complexity |
| AI coach persona (Sage) | Personalized, empathetic health guidance |
| WhatsApp + Mobile App channels | Meet users where they are |

## Artifacts Created

- [Executive Vision Document](../Executive-Vision-Document.md)

## Context

yHealth serves as the foundation platform for Xyric's health technology portfolio. It establishes the core AI coaching patterns that can be extended to more specialized applications like DR AI Healthcare.
